Loss of Coral Reefs

Action Plan

Goal: To enforce regulations that recover , sustain, and protect coral reef ecosystems

  1. Conduct an in-depth assessment of coral reef health to identify priority conservation areas. (2 months)
  2. Review and strengthen coral reef protection laws and enforce stricter penalties for activities harmful to coral reefs, especially to Marine Protected Areas. (6 months)
  3. Collaborate with local and coastal communities to increase awareness about the importance of coral reefs while encouraging sustainable practices like responsible fishing and eco-friendly tourism. (2 months)
  4. Begin a monitoring program to regularly track coral reef health. (4 months)
  5. Establish research collaborations to support ongoing scientific efforts in coral reef conservation and restoration. (6 months)
  6. Launch coral restoration projects and initiatives that involve local communities and NGOs. (6 months)
